Info

A website aimed at matching UWA students seeking a group or partner to study (or complete a project) with. Users are simply matched automatically by the system, as opposed to the general concept of two users "Liking" or "Confirming" each other before a match is made in typical matchmaking sites. The idea is to build a network of contacts who you can comfortably study with across all your units.

Users only need to login via LMS.
Unit's, student number/email, name, can all be pulled from the LMS API. The algorithm will only match based of two criteria: unit preferences, availability preferences (specified by the user).
